# Stockly - Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

**Name**: Stockly  
**Tagline**: "Stock Management Made Simple"  
**Industry**: B2B Warehouse & Inventory Management  
**Positioning**: Modern, efficient, user-friendly supply chain platform

---

## Logo

### Primary Logo
- Icon + Wordmark combination
- Icon: Stacked boxes forming "S" with upward arrow
- Represents: Growth, organization, efficiency

### Logo Variations
1. **Full Logo**: Icon + "Stockly" text (primary use)
2. **Icon Only**: For app icons, favicons, small spaces
3. **Wordmark Only**: For text-heavy contexts

### Clear Space
- Maintain minimum clear space of 1x the icon height around logo
- Never stretch, rotate, or distort the logo

## Typography

### Primary Font
**Inter** - Modern, clean, highly readable  
- Headings: 600-700 weight
- Body: 400-500 weight
- Small text: 400 weight

### Font Sizes
```css
--text-xs: 0.75rem     /* 12px */
--text-sm: 0.875rem    /* 14px */

--text-3xl: 1.875rem   /* 30px */
--text-4xl: 2.25rem    /* 36px */

```

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice
- **Professional** yet approachable
- **Clear** and straightforward
- **Confident** but not arrogant
- **Helpful** and supportive

### Writing Style
- Use active voice
- Keep sentences concise
- Avoid jargon when possible
- Be direct and honest

### Example Phrases
✅ "Manage your inventory effortlessly"  
✅ "Real-time insights at your fingertips"  
✅ "Streamline your warehouse operations"  
❌ "Leverage synergies to optimize workflows"  
❌ "Paradigm-shifting solutions"

## UI Components

### Buttons
- **Primary**: Cyan background, white text
- **Secondary**: Transparent with cyan border
- **Ghost**: No background, cyan text
- Border radius: 0.75rem (12px)
- Padding: 0.75rem 1.5rem

### Cards
- Background: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.05)
- Border: 1px solid rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1)
- Border radius: 1rem (16px)
- Backdrop blur: 12px

### Icons
- Style: Outline/stroke icons (Lucide React)
- Size: 16px, 20px, 24px (based on context)
- Color: Inherit from text or use brand colors

## Imagery

### Photography Style
- Clean, modern warehouse environments
- Well-lit, organized spaces
- Technology in use
- Diverse team members
- Avoid cluttered or chaotic scenes

### Illustrations
- Geometric, minimal style
- Use brand colors
- Flat design aesthetic
- Consistent line weight

## Application

### Website
- Dark theme by default
- Cyan accents throughout
- Generous white space
- Clear hierarchy

### Dashboard
- Data visualization in brand colors
- Consistent card layouts
- Intuitive navigation
- Real-time updates highlighted

### Marketing Materials
- Logo prominently displayed
- Consistent color usage
- Professional imagery
- Clear call-to-actions

---

## Do's and Don'ts

### ✅ Do
- Use the official logo files
- Maintain color consistency
- Keep designs clean and minimal
- Ensure high contrast for readability
- Use approved fonts

### ❌ Don't
- Alter logo colors
- Use outdated logo versions
- Crowd the logo with other elements
- Use low-resolution images
- Mix different design styles
